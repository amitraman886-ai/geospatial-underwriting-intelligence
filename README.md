# 🌍 Geospatial Underwriting Intelligence Platform

A web-based underwriting decision support application that enables insurers and insurance professionals to perform **postcode-level geospatial risk assessments across the UK**.

The platform allows users to enter a UK postcode and automatically retrieves publicly available environmental and geospatial datasets to generate an underwriting risk profile, risk index, key insights, and suggested underwriting considerations.

## 📌 Project Overview

Traditional property underwriting often requires underwriters to consult multiple external data sources before assessing location-based risk.

This application demonstrates how publicly available geospatial intelligence can be integrated into a single underwriting dashboard to support faster and more informed underwriting decisions.

By entering a UK postcode, the platform automatically:

- Retrieves location information
- Collects multiple environmental and geospatial datasets
- Analyses exposure indicators
- Calculates a composite risk score
- Generates underwriting insights
- Provides practical underwriting considerations

# 🖥️ Features

### 📍 Postcode-Based Risk Assessment

Users simply enter a UK postcode.

The application automatically identifies the location and retrieves relevant environmental and geospatial information.

---

### 🌦 Weather Risk Analysis

Provides historical weather indicators including:

- Average temperature
- Maximum temperature
- Minimum temperature
- Wind exposure
- Annual rainfall
- Weather extremes

The application highlights potential underwriting concerns such as:

- Freeze exposure
- Wind damage
- Surface water accumulation
- Damp and mould exposure

---

### 🚔 Crime Risk Assessment

Analyses publicly available crime statistics to identify:

- Recent crime activity
- Crime trends
- Area crime exposure

---

### 🏘 Area Risk Assessment

Evaluates:

- Urban density
- Average property values

Provides underwriting insights regarding exposure and portfolio characteristics.

---

### 🌊 Flood Risk Assessment

Reviews:

- Elevation
- Flood susceptibility indicators

Highlights potential flood-related underwriting considerations.

---

### 🌍 Ground & Subsidence Risk

Assesses:

- Soil type
- Clay content
- Subsidence sensitivity
- Heave potential

Provides practical underwriting observations relating to ground movement and structural stability.

---

### 📊 Composite Risk Index

The platform combines multiple environmental indicators into a single **Composite Risk Index**.

The overall location is classified as:

- Low Risk
- Moderate Risk
- High Risk

This provides an easy-to-understand summary of overall underwriting exposure.

---

### 💡 Executive Underwriting Summary

The application automatically generates:

- Top risk drivers
- Executive summary
- Suggested underwriting considerations

These insights are intended to support underwriting triage and risk awareness.

---

# 🧠 Risk Methodology

The application combines publicly available environmental and geospatial information to estimate relative underwriting exposure.

Risk indicators include:

- Weather patterns
- Rainfall intensity
- Temperature extremes
- Wind exposure
- Crime activity
- Flood indicators
- Ground conditions
- Soil characteristics
- Area demographics

Each factor contributes to a composite underwriting risk score.

The platform is designed as a decision-support tool and should not replace insurer-specific underwriting guidelines.
